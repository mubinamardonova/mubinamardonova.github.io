Catch me if you can

Directed by: Steven Spielberg

Produced by: Steven Spielberg Walter F. Parkes

Screenplay by: Jeff Nathanson

Based on: Catch Me If You Can by Frank Abagnale Jr.Stan Redding

Release date: December 25, 2002

Country: United States

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio and Tom Hanks, with Christopher Walken, Martin Sheen, and Nathalie Baye in supporting roles.

Budget: $52 million

Box office: $352.1 million

Plot: A seasoned FBI agent pursues Frank Abagnale Jr. who, before his 19th birthday, successfully forged millions of dollars' worth of checks while posing as a Pan Am pilot, a doctor, and a legal prosecutor.
